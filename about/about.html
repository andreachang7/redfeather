About

Red Feather sits in the heart of Greenwich village, close to the lively and beautiful Washington Square Park. The comfortable dining area will evoke a fun yet upscale atmosphere with beautiful colors and art pieces from the ceiling that will pair with the craftsmanship from the Chef and bartender.

Chef Pia Gervacio

Main Chef

Red Feather is helmed by Chef Pia Gervacio who has trained at and managed several Michelin starred restaurants. Having been born and raised in the Philippines, she will bring all the vibrant flavors of Southeast Asia (Thailand, Philippines, Indonesia, Korea, Japan) and pair it with Modern American fare.
